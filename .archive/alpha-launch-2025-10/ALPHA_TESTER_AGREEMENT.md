## 4. Data & Privacy

### 4.1 Your Writing Content

**Ownership:**

- You retain full ownership of all content you create in Inkwell
- We do not claim any rights to your writing

**Storage:**

- Your content is stored locally on your device (IndexedDB)
- Authentication data (email, password) is stored on Supabase (encrypted)
- We do not access, read, or store your writing content on our servers

### 4.2 Telemetry & Analytics (Optional)

If you enable telemetry, we collect:

- Anonymous session data (session ID, duration)
- Feature usage (e.g., "exported PDF", "created chapter")
- Performance metrics (e.g., load time, autosave latency)

**We do NOT collect:**

- Your writing content
- Project names or descriptions
- Character names or story details
- Personally identifiable information (beyond email for authentication)

**Opt-Out:**

- Settings → Privacy → Toggle "Enable Telemetry" OFF

### 4.3 Feedback & Bug Reports

When you submit feedback or bug reports:

- We may store your email and communication history
- We may store screenshots or diagnostic data you provide
- We will not share your personal information with third parties

### 4.4 Data Retention

**During Alpha:**

- Your account and data remain active throughout the alpha period
- You can export or delete your data anytime

**After Alpha:**

- Your account will transition to closed beta (if invited)
- You can delete your account anytime via Settings
